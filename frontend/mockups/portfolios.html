Shadow NAV methodology: Same starting capital as actual book on 01 Jan 2026. Every rec emitted is assumed executed at close of rec day, net of standard brokerage + STT/stamp. Dividends reinvested same-day. No slippage assumption beyond 5 bps/side. Shadow has zero discretionary trades — only system calls. Actual NAV = FM's actual IBKR / Kotak Neo positions reconciled daily at 08:42 IST. Delta = shadow − actual, decomposed into skipped-call drag (recs not actioned that would have lifted NAV) and discretion contribution (FM trades not in the system's feed).
What the banner number really says: +68 bps YTD means if you had followed every rec blindly and done nothing else, the aggregate book would be 68 bps higher today. It's the cost of discretion, not the system's claimed alpha over bench. System's alpha over bench sits in each book's YTD vs bench tile.
