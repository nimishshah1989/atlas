"Defensive rotation in progress — money is moving to quality and visibility as broad-market breadth thins."

The current correction regime has produced a textbook defensive rotation: Pharma (+1.8 RS) and Defence (+2.1 RS) are firmly in the LEADING quadrant with improving momentum and broad internal participation — over 70% of stocks in both sectors are trading above their 50-day moving averages. Crucially, both sectors are also outperforming MCX Gold INR, earning the AMPLIFIES_BULL classification. This is the highest-conviction signal in the ATLAS framework: a sector simultaneously beating the equity benchmark and gold implies genuine strength, not merely a flight-to-safety bid. Position sizing in these two sectors may be scaled to 1.5× normal sector weight, subject to portfolio mandate limits.

FMCG (+0.3 RS) and Bank (-0.4 RS) have both entered the WEAKENING quadrant — their RS scores remain elevated relative to laggards, but four-week momentum is now negative. This is an early-warning signal. The typical RRG pattern is clockwise rotation: sectors exit LEADING into WEAKENING before migrating into LAGGING. FMCG's transition is particularly worth monitoring: consumer staples historically hold up during corrections, but with rural demand softening and input costs rising, the sector may be losing its defensive premium. Watch for RS momentum to turn negative two consecutive weeks — that would confirm the transition to LAGGING and warrant a trim to below-benchmark weight.

Metal and Realty are in the IMPROVING quadrant — both show negative RS scores but rising RS momentum, suggesting the rate of underperformance is slowing. However, breadth has not confirmed this improvement: only 43% of Metal stocks and 32% of Realty stocks are above their 50-DMA, well below the 50% threshold ATLAS uses as a breadth confirmation gate. IT (-1.4 RS, -0.8 momentum) remains the clearest LAGGING sector — global tech demand uncertainty, rupee headwinds, and rising offshore competition have combined into a sustained de-rating cycle. All four factors are red. Reduce or avoid. Do not enter Metal or Realty until breadth crosses 50% — the IMPROVING signal is a watch, not an entry.
