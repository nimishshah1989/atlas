Methodology — Signal Playback Simulator

Show / hide

The Signal Playback Simulator back-tests a systematic strategy where buy and sell decisions are driven entirely by market breadth readings (count of Nifty 500 stocks above the 21-EMA). Capital allocation rules are fully configurable.

Capital Inputs: Initial lump sum is deployed immediately. Monthly SIP is invested on the first trading day of each month unless breadth is in the overbought zone. Additional lumpsum can be deployed at breadth oversold signals.

Exit Rules: When breadth reaches the overbought threshold (≥400), a configurable percentage of the portfolio is sold. If breadth continues above the further-sell level, another tranche is trimmed.

Redeploy Rules: Cash deployed back into the market when breadth falls below configurable redeployment thresholds — systematic bottom-fishing guided by data.

Tax Treatment: FIFO units. Pre 2024-07-23 — LTCG 10% (holding >12 months), STCG 15%. Post 2024-07-23 — LTCG 12.5%, STCG 20%. Grandfathering of pre-January 2018 unrealised gains is applied.
